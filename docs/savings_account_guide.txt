SAVINGS ACCOUNT PRODUCT GUIDE
Meridian National Bank (sample / fictional)

OVERVIEW
The Meridian Everyday Savings Account is a deposit account designed for
individual customers who want to set money aside while earning interest.
It is a fictional product used here only for demonstration.

ELIGIBILITY
- Available to resident individuals aged 18 and above.
- Minors aged 10 and above may open an account jointly with a guardian.
- A valid government-issued identity document and address proof are required
  to satisfy Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements.

INTEREST
- Interest rate: 3.50% per annum on balances up to 100,000 units.
- Interest rate: 4.00% per annum on the portion of the balance above 100,000 units.
- Interest is calculated on the daily closing balance and credited quarterly.

MINIMUM BALANCE
- Minimum average monthly balance: 5,000 units.
- A service charge of 150 units applies in any month the average balance
  falls below this minimum.

FEES
- Account opening: free.
- Quarterly account statement: free (electronic).
- Physical statement on request: 50 units per statement.
- Replacement debit card: 200 units.

DIGITAL ACCESS
Customers can access the account through the Meridian mobile app and internet
banking. Both channels support balance enquiry, fund transfers, bill payments,
and statement downloads. Two-factor authentication is mandatory for all
login sessions and for transactions above 10,000 units.

NOMINATION
Account holders are encouraged to register a nominee. Nomination can be done
at account opening or later through any branch or the internet banking portal.

CLOSURE
An account may be closed by submitting a written request at the branch. Any
accrued interest is paid up to the date of closure. Accounts inactive for more
than 24 months are classified as dormant and require re-activation through KYC
re-verification before further transactions are allowed.
